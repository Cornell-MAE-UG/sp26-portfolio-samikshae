Usability of design: The nutcracker design is highly usable because the linear actuator provides a consistent force well above the 80 lbf required, eliminating reliance on human grip strength. The actuator’s 3.1-inch stroke is sufficient to fully close the jaws around a macadamia nut, making the device easy to operate and reliable for repeated use.
